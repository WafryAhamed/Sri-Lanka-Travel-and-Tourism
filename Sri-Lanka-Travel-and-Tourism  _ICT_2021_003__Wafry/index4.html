Mirissa

Mirissa is a charming coastal town in Sri Lanka, located in the Matara District of the Southern Province. Known for its picturesque beach, vibrant nightlife, and as a prime whale and dolphin watching spot, Mirissa offers a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure. The town boasts a rich fishing heritage, with tuna, mullet, snapper, and butterfish being popular catches.
